Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make a reservation?

You can book an appointment through our website by filling out the reservation form. We'll get back to you within 24 hours to confirm your appointment. Alternatively, you can contact us via LINE, WhatsApp, or email.

What is your cancellation policy?

We understand that plans change. Please let us know at least 24 hours in advance if you need to cancel or reschedule. For cancellations with less than 24 hours notice, a 20% fee may apply if a deposit was made.

Is tipping customary?

Tipping is not required or expected in Korea, but it is appreciated if you are particularly satisfied with the service. Our priority is your satisfaction, not additional compensation.

What happens if I'm late for my appointment?

We understand that navigating a foreign city can be challenging. Please try to arrive on time, but if you're running late, call us as soon as possible. We'll try our best to accommodate you, but if you're more than 15 minutes late, we may need to reschedule or adjust your service.

Do your stylists speak English?

Yes, all of our stylists speak English at varying levels of proficiency. Some of our stylists also speak Japanese and Chinese. We'll make sure to match you with a stylist who can communicate with you effectively.

How can I share what style I want?

We recommend bringing photos of the styles you like. Our stylists are experienced in working with international clients and will take time to understand exactly what you want. Feel free to share reference images when booking or during your consultation.
